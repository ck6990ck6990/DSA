Problem definition:

    Calculate the inverse of a square matrix by Gaussian elimination.


----------------------------

Input:

n

a11 a12 ... a1n

a21 a22 ... a2n

.

.

.

an1 an2 ... ann

n is the dimension of the input matrix.
a is the input matrix

-------------------------------

Output:

e

b11 b12 ... b1n

.

.

.

bn1 bn2 ... bnn

e is the error between matrix (a*b) and matrix I.

b is the inverse matrix of a.
